Shrimp Fried Rice


Description

Shrimp Fried Rice is a Chinese dish that was created to use leftover rice, consisting of cooked/leftover rice, shrimp, scrambled eggs, and vegetables like peas, carrots and onions that are all stir-fried together within a hot wok. A quick and customizable staple, you're not going to want to stop eating this!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 11/2cups uncooked white rice
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup fresh bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked medium shrimp (peeled, deveined w/o tail)
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes. Set aside and allow rice to completely cool.
  3. Heat a large skillet or wok for 2 minutes. When the skillet or wok is hot, pour in vegetable oil, bean sprouts, and onions. Mix well and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Mix in cooled rice and shrimp and cook for another 3 minutes, stirring continuously.
  5. Add in eggs, green onion, soy sauce, salt, pepper, and sesame oil. Cook for another 4 minutes, stirring continuously, until eggs are cooked and everything is blended evenly.
